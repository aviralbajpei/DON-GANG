About

What is the Smart AI
Sustainable Mirror?

An AI-powered mirror deployed in clothing stores, creating a bridge between cutting-edge technology and conscious fashion.

AI Vision Scan

An integrated camera and AI engine scans you in seconds, capturing 200+ data points about your unique features.

Tone & Type Detection

Deep learning algorithms detect your precise skin tone and classify your body type with clinical accuracy.

Style Recommendations

Get curated clothing suggestions tailored exclusively for your body — colors, cuts, and silhouettes that truly flatter.

Sustainability Score

See the carbon footprint of every recommendation and discover eco-friendly alternatives to make conscious choices.

Process

How It Works

Four intelligent steps from scan to sustainable style, in under 10 seconds.

01

AI Scanning

The mirror's camera activates and uses computer vision to scan your face and body with precision depth mapping.

02

AI Analysis

Deep neural networks analyze skin undertones and classify body shape using 500+ trained body-type parameters.

03

Personalized Picks

The system cross-references the store's inventory to surface clothing that flatters your unique measurements and tone.

04

Sustainability Score

Each recommendation displays a sustainability score, CO₂ footprint, and greener alternatives to empower conscious buying.

Features
