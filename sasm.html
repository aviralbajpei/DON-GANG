About

What is the Smart AI
Sustainable Mirror?

An intelligent mirror installed in clothing stores that uses computer vision and AI to create a deeply personal, eco-conscious shopping experience for every single customer.

AI Vision Scan

An integrated camera and deep learning engine scans your face and body in seconds, capturing over 200 unique biometric data points.

Skin Tone Analysis

Advanced spectral imaging detects precise skin undertones across all ethnicities and skin types with 99.1% accuracy.

Style Recommendations

Curated outfit suggestions matched to your unique body shape — colors, cuts, and silhouettes that genuinely flatter you.

Eco Impact Display

Every recommendation shows its carbon footprint, sustainability score, and certified eco-friendly alternatives to empower your choices.

Process

Four Steps from Scan
to Sustainable Style.

The entire experience takes under 10 seconds. Here's the magic behind it.

01

AI Scanning

Step in front of the mirror. The camera activates instantly and begins a full-body scan using depth-sensing computer vision.

02

AI Analysis

Neural networks analyze your skin undertone and classify your body type using 500+ trained shape parameters.

03

Personalized Picks

The system cross-references live store inventory to surface clothing in the exact cuts, colors, and styles that suit you best.

04

Sustainability Report

See a full eco-report — CO₂ footprint, sustainability score, and certified organic or recycled-fabric alternatives.

Body Type Analysis

NEW

Virtual Try-On

Color Matching

Carbon Footprint

Eco Alternatives

Retail Analytics

Anonymized trend data, dwell time, and conversion metrics to help stores optimize inventory.
